Private & Local

Runs OpenAI Whisper locally on the Apple Neural Engine. Your audio never leaves your Mac. Zero telemetry.

✨

AI Summaries

Auto-generate bulleted summaries of your notes using local LLMs (Ollama) or your favorite cloud provider.

🚀

Global Hotkey

Press Ctrl+0 and speak in any app — Slack, Notion, Teams, or Safari. Text appears live at your cursor.
